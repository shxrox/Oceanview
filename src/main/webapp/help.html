System Guide

1. Making a Booking

Navigate to "New Booking" from the dashboard. Select your check-in and check-out dates. The system will show only available rooms. Click "Book Now" and enter the guest details.

2. Managing Reservations

The dashboard shows all active bookings. To cancel a booking, simply click the red "Cancel" button on the right side of the row.

3. Email Confirmations

Since this is a demo system, confirmation emails are not sent over the internet. Instead, they are saved as text files in the FakeEmails folder on the server.
