Getting Started

Welcome to the Ocean View Resort Management System. This software helps manage room bookings, guest invoices, and staff accounts efficiently.

Logging In

Access the system using your email and password.

  • Admin: Has full access to revenue reports and user management.
  • Receptionist: Can search rooms, make bookings, and print invoices.

Making a Booking

Follow these steps to reserve a room for a guest:

Step 1: Check Availability

From the dashboard, click "üîç New Booking".

  • Select a Check-In Date.
  • Select a Check-Out Date (must be after check-in).
  • Click "Search Rooms".

Note: The system automatically hides dates that are in the past.

Step 2: Select a Room

You will see a list of available rooms (Deluxe, Standard, Suite). Click the green "Book Now" button next to the desired room.

Step 3: Guest Details

Enter the guest's Name, Email, and Phone Number. The system will automatically calculate the Total Price based on the number of nights.

Receipts & Emails

üìÑ Printed Invoices

After confirming a booking, an invoice is automatically generated.

  • Click "Print Invoice" to send it to the printer or save as PDF.
  • You can reprint invoices later from the Dashboard table.

üìß Email Confirmations

The system sends a digital receipt to the guest automatically.

NOTE Since this is a demo environment, emails are not sent over the internet. Instead, they are saved as text files in the FakeEmails folder on the server computer.

  1. Click the purple "üë§ My Profile" card on your dashboard.

Next time you login, use your new password.

Troubleshooting

"Invalid Email or Password"

Double-check your spelling. If you forgot your password, ask an Admin to delete your account and create a new one.

"Error Loading Data"

If charts or tables don't load, the server might be down. Contact IT Support to restart the Tomcat Server.

Check-Out Date Issue

Ensure the Check-Out date is strictly after the Check-In date. You cannot book a room for 0 nights.
