Receipts & Bookings Guide

This guide explains how to generate, print, and manage guest invoices and bookings efficiently.

1. Generating a Receipt

Receipts are automatically created when a booking is confirmed.

Step 1: Go to "New Booking".
Step 2: Select check-in and check-out dates.
Step 3: Choose a room and enter guest details.
Step 4: Click "Confirm & Pay" to generate the receipt page.

2. Printing or Saving Invoices

  • Print: Click "Print Invoice" and select your printer.
  • Save as PDF: In the print dialog, select "Save as PDF" as the destination.

3. Reprinting Old Receipts

To reprint, check the "Recent Reservations" table on the dashboard. Click the "Action" button or search by guest ID.

Ensure you have the correct guest and reservation date before reprinting.

4. Email Confirmations

The system sends a simulated email receipt to the guest automatically.

NOTE Emails are not sent over the internet in this demo environment.

  • Find them in Documents/OceanView/FakeEmails/.
  • Open the corresponding text file to view the content.

5. Troubleshooting

  • Invoice shows $0.00? Check that the check-out date is after the check-in date.
  • Printer issues? Verify your computer's printer settings; the print button triggers the browser print dialog.
